David Tagatac


Hello world, I am a computer science graduate student at Columbia University in the City of New York. I am currently investigating the effects of synchronization scheduling on concurrent program insecurity. Before joining Columbia in 2012, I assisted the Suomi NPP weather satellite ground systems team with large data flow and distributed processing support.

In a previous life, I was a condensed matter physicist at UCSD, earning a Materials Science M.S. there in 2007, and an Engineering Physics B.S. at Cornell University before that in 2006.
